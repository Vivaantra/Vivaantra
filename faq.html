Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Vivaantra, our programs, and the application process.

We've compiled a list of common inquiries to help you get the information you need. If you don't find your question here, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

What is Vivaantra's core mission?

Vivaantra's core mission is to empower individuals from social science backgrounds with essential AI knowledge and practical skills, enabling them to drive meaningful societal impact and accelerate their careers at the intersection of human understanding and technology.

Who are Vivaantra's programs designed for?

Our programs are designed for social scientists, policymakers, researchers, educators, non-profit professionals, and aspiring individuals who want to integrate AI and data science into their social science expertise.

What kind of prerequisites do your courses have?

Many of our foundational courses have no specific technical prerequisites, only a strong interest in AI's application in social sciences. Specialized courses may require a basic understanding of AI fundamentals or statistical concepts, as noted in their descriptions.

What is the learning format for Vivaantra programs?

Our programs are primarily online, offering a blend of self-paced modules, live interactive sessions, hands-on projects, and discussion forums to accommodate diverse learning styles and schedules.

Do you offer career support or job placement assistance?

While we don't guarantee job placement, our programs focus heavily on practical skills and portfolio building. We also provide career guidance resources, networking opportunities, and workshops on resume building and interviewing for AI-related roles in the social sector.

How can I enroll in a program?

You can browse our programs on the 'Programs' page. Each course has a link to an inquiry form or an enrollment page. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via the 'Contact' page, and our admissions team will assist you.
